John p. Stevens Model United Nations

Dear Delegates and Advisors, 

It is my pleasure to cordially invite you to attend the annual spring John P. Stevens Model United Nations Conference, which will be hosted on May 9, 2015. I hope that you choose to be a part of the JPSMUN experience as it is an engaging and exciting learning opportunity for both accomplished and novice delegates alike.

As one of the largest and finest single-day conferences on the east coast, JPSMUN provides a unique occasion for delegates to display and refine their skills. For over twenty years, John P. Stevens has carried a rich legacy; the debate offered at JPSMUN carries unmatched eminence, gravity, and excellence. Our simulation emphasizes the value of diplomacy and compromise, while maintaining a competitive and stimulating Model UN environment. 

We are thrilled to present four committees, including a General Assembly, a crisis, and economic and social committees. These simulations range from historical settings to futuristic ones, all of which are sure to bring out the best in delegates.

Registration for the conference is now open, and  under the Conference Registration tab. JPSMUN will be held at John P. Stevens High School, and will be a day-long engagement. 

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us by phone (Monday-Friday, 5-10 pm) at 732-318-4580 or by e-mail atjpsmun2@gmail.com.  We look forward to getting in touch with you soon and see you next November!

On behalf of the entire JPSMUN Secretariat, 

Jessica Francis 
Secretary- General

RUMUN

This past weekend, JPSMUN attended RUMUN 2014. Congratulations to all of JPSMUN on delivering an incredible performance. This is a great way to start of the year and is only an indication for even greater things to come in the future. With 4 Best Delegate awards, 1 Outstanding, 1 Most Improved, and 2 Position Paper Commendations, JPSMUN clearly left its mark on every single person present at RUMUN. A special thanks to our advisers Ms. Peterson and Ms. Schiavone; we would not be anywhere without them. 

For those of you that did not receive an award, use this conference as a learning experience. If you stay persistent and build upon your skills, success awaits you in the future. Model UN is a process- use your failures to fuel your future aspirations. However, regardless of whether or not you received an award you were part of the team effort and that is what you should be most proud of. This weekend we proved that JPSMUN truly is a family unlike any other and we  carried on the legacy that has been passed down to us from all the greats that have participated in this club.  Great job to everyone again for proving the talent that is JPSMUN.
