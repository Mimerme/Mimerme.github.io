Staying Still is one of my first games designed
around a game mechanic were the world
can rotate, giving the player a new puzzle and
new enviornment to work with 4 times in each level.
It was developed with another experimental player controller
inspired by Super Meat Boy. It is for
Mac and Windows, with a possible
Linux build. You can also find it on Desura.
